ABOUT ME

While pursuing my degree in exercise science, I developed an interest in data and optimization and began creating training programs for friends and family. In the fitness field, my approach using data and evidence-based, scientific programming deviated from the norm. The systems were not user-friendly or scientific. This led me to learn software development while working at one of the biggest fitness companies, Equinox. I created a full-stack responsive web application that I implemented with my clients. My knowledge of scientific programming and software engineering created something that many could benefit from. I look forward to bringing my knowledge, work ethic, and communication skills to other companies and clients, for their web development needs.
